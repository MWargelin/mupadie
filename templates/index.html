Mupadie

Welcome! Mupadie is an application to test and evaluate musical pattern discovery algorithms. Start by uploading a piece of music as a MIDI file with the form above to visualise musical patterns in that piece.

The Algorithms

There are two algorithms implemented in Mupadie. They are:

  • SIATEC, presented in Meredith, David, Kjell Lemström, and Geraint A. Wiggins. "Algorithms for discovering repeated patterns in multidimensional representations of polyphonic music." Journal of New Music Research 31.4 (2002): 321-345.
  • transposition and time-warp invariant algorithm, presented in Laaksonen, Antti, and Kjell Lemström. "Transposition and time-warp invariant algorithm for detecting repeated patterns in polyphonic music." 6th International Conference on Digital Libraries for Musicology. 2019.

You can also upload your own musical pattern discovery results as a JSON file, if you want to visualise patterns found by an algorithm not included in this application! See further instructions uploading JSON files.
